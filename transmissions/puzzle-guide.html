Neuko Puzzle Guide ​

"The joy comes from the exploration of the unknown."

Puzzles are the core engagement engine of Neuko. They are not just fun diversions; they are social experiments designed to test collaboration, conviction, and attention to detail.

Philosophy ​

  • No Cheat Sheet: The team will never give a direct answer. Discovery is the product.
  • Collaborative: Puzzles are often too hard for one person. They require the "hive mind" to solve.
  • Integrated: Real community members are often woven into the puzzles (e.g., receiving codes to distribute).

Common Techniques ​

If you want to be an Operative, you need to know how to look.

1. Audio Spectrograms ​

What to look for: Images hidden in sound waves. How to solve: Download the video's audio and run it through a spectrogram viewer (like Audacity or online tools). History: First discovered by @0xstateoftheart in Post #5.

2. Visual Contrast Codes ​

What to look for: Hidden text that only appears when brightness/contrast is manipulated. How to solve: Take screenshots of dark or glitchy scenes. Crank up the exposure and contrast in Photoshop/GIMP. History: Used in Post #7 to hide code words like "HOOTS-49".

3. Website Console Logs ​

What to look for: Hidden messages in the browser's developer console. How to solve: Go to neuko.ai, right-click -> Inspect -> Console. Look for text logs or hidden .txt files. History: Discovered by @thrax_nft, revealing G*Boy's escape logs.

4. Frame-by-Frame Analysis ​

What to look for: Single-frame glitches containing numbers, letters, or QR codes. How to solve: Download the video and step through it frame by frame.

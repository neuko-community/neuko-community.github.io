# Neuko AI Puzzles

> Complete catalog of puzzles, solutions, and puzzle-solving techniques used in Neuko AI.

## Overview

Puzzles are core to Neuko AI's engagement strategy. Hidden in videos, audio, website code, and community interactions, they unlock lore, reward engagement, and create a collaborative problem-solving culture. The team designs puzzles as social experiments, decentralizing participation and testing community conviction.

## Puzzle Types

### 1. Spectrograms (Audio Analysis)

**Description**: Images hidden in sound waves, visible when analyzing audio frequency spectrums.

**Discovery Method**: 
- Use audio analysis tools (spectrogram viewers)
- Analyze audio tracks from videos
- Look for visual patterns in frequency data

**Examples**:
- **Post #5 (Distribution Complete)**: Spectrograms reveal:
  - Time machine imagery
  - Testing chamber with badge on device
- **Discovery**: Found by @0xstateoftheart using audio tools

**Tools Used**:
- Audio spectrogram viewers
- Frequency analysis software
- Audio editing tools

**Source**: `sources/thread-4.md`, `sources/info-dump.md`

## Puzzle-Solving Techniques

### General Approach

1. **Watch Multiple Times**: 
   - First watch for enjoyment
   - Second watch for puzzles
   - Frame-by-frame analysis

2. **Multiple Tools**: 
   - Don't rely on single method
   - Try spectral, visual, audio, code analysis
   - Combine techniques

3. **Community Collaboration**: 
   - Share discoveries
   - Build on others' findings
   - Document solutions

4. **Look for Patterns**: 
   - Connect elements across videos
   - Recognize community references
   - Follow numerical or coded patterns

### Specific Techniques

**Spectrogram Analysis**:
- Extract audio from video
- Use spectrogram viewer tool
- Look for visual patterns in frequency data
- May reveal images or text

**Contrast Adjustment**:
- Extract frames from video
- Adjust contrast/brightness in image editor
- Look for hidden text or patterns
- Compare before/after

**Console Inspection**:
- Open browser developer tools (F12)
- Inspect website source code
- Check console for logs or errors
- Extract .txt files if present

**Frame-by-Frame Analysis**:
- Extract individual frames from video
- Screenshot key moments
- Analyze backgrounds and details
- Look for codes, numbers, or patterns

**Community Recognition**:
- Know community memes and inside jokes
- Recognize member references
- Connect visual/audio to specific people
- Follow clues to community members

## Puzzle Philosophy

### Design Principles

**Social Experiments**: Puzzles designed to test community collaboration and conviction

**Decentralized Participation**: Community members involved in puzzle distribution and solving

**Reward Engagement**: Puzzles reward those who pay attention and contribute

**No Cheat Sheet**: Team emphasizes exploration and discovery over explicit instructions

**Collaborative Storytelling**: Puzzles weave community members into narrative

### Team Approach

- **Watching Community**: Team monitors engagement to curate puzzle distribution
- **Involving Members**: Real community members receive codes to distribute
- **Rewarding Solvers**: Shoutouts and recognition for puzzle contributors
- **Evolving Complexity**: Puzzles escalate from simple to multi-layer challenges
